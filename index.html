About

Smart India Hackathon Project

This project — Smart Tourist Safety Monitoring & Incident Response — is an entry for the Smart India Hackathon (SIH). It demonstrates blockchain-based temporary tourist IDs, AI anomaly detection, geo-fencing alerts, and rapid incident response for safer tourism. Team: [Your Team Name]. Mentor: Samuel Satwik.

The STS-IRS system addresses the critical need for tourist safety in India's growing tourism sector. By combining cutting-edge technologies with practical safety measures, we aim to reduce response times during emergencies and provide tourists with peace of mind during their travels.
